# Research: Popular Roblox Pet Simulator Games -- Feature Comparison
**Date**: 2026-04-09
**Purpose**: Competitive analysis for simulator generation pipeline improvements

---

## 1. PETS

### Pet Simulator 99 (PS99)
- **Total pets**: 2,000+ unique pets
- **Rarity tiers (ascending)**: Common > Uncommon > Rare > Epic > Legendary > Mythical > Exclusive > Huge > Titanic > Gargantuan
- **Size categories**: Normal < Huge < Titanic < Gargantuan
  - Huge: chance to hatch from eggs (unlocked after Rebirth 2)
  - Titanic: 2x stronger than player's best pets, rideable
  - Gargantuan: larger and stronger than Titanic, each has a serial number and username of the hatcher; variants have their own serials
- **Variants**: Golden, Rainbow, Shiny
  - Golden: +0.3x more damage than Normal
  - Rainbow: +0.9x more damage than Normal
  - Shiny: +1.0x more damage (2x normal)
  - All three variants apply to Huge, Titanic, and Gargantuan pets too
- **Leveling**: Huge/Titanic/Gargantuan pets earn XP and level up to level 99, enhancing damage by up to +100%
- **Fusing**: Fuse Machine accepts 3-100 pets into one stronger pet (all except Secret and Exclusive). Fusing Mastery levels up to guarantee gold/rainbow, chance for 2-for-1, minimum reduced from 3 to 2
- **Enchantments**: 27 total enchantments (9 General, 18 Special)
  - Enchant slots unlock at ranks 3, 5, 8, 11
  - Standard enchants upgradeable via Upgrade Enchants Machine (costs diamonds + lower tier enchants)
  - Stacking with diminishing returns (100% > 60% > 38.3% > 27.5%)
  - Key enchants: Coins IX, Diamonds X, Lucky, Treasure Hunter, Speed V, Criticals, Strong Pets, Tap Power
  - Unique enchants: Huge Hunter, Midas Touch, Fortune, Exotic Pets, Shiny Hunter, Lucky Blocks, Chest Mimic, Massive Comet, Diamond Chest Mimic
  - Magic Orb, Fireworks, Midas Touch, Magnet do NOT diminish when stacked

### Pet Simulator X (PSX) -- discontinued
- **Rarity tiers (8)**: Basic > Rare > Epic > Legendary > Mythical > Secret > Exclusive (+ Huge)
- **Enchantment capacity by rarity**:
  - Below Mythical: up to 2 enchants
  - Mythical/Secret: 3 enchants (at least 1 unique)
  - Exclusive: up to 4 enchants
- **Enchant levels**: I through V (5 being highest)
- **Legendary+ pets**: automatically get enchants on hatch
- **Fusing**: at Beach area or Trading Plaza. Mythical/Exclusive/Secret/Event pets cannot be fused. Fusing Legendary re-enchants with new enchants

### Arm Wrestle Simulator
- **Pet variants**: 3 variants (Regular, Golden 1.5x strength, Void -- converted from Golden)
- **Pet sizes + mutations**: mergeable with variants
- **Storage**: starts at 70, equip max 3 initially (expandable via tokens/Robux/index/super rebirth upgrades/events)
- **Pets boost**: strength multiplier, rarer = better multiplier

### Pet Catchers
- **Total pets**: 300+ discoverable/catchable pets
- **Catching mechanic**: uses Cubes (not eggs), different cube types for different rarities
- **4 cube types**: basic through premium, better cube = higher catch rate

## 2. EGGS

### Pet Simulator 99
- **Total eggs**: 281+ (as of Update 68)
- **Categories**: World Eggs, Exclusive Eggs, Fishing World Eggs
- **Multi-hatch system**: Egg Machine unlocks at Area 8
  - Can hatch up to 99 eggs simultaneously
  - Each new tier costs more diamonds
  - Recommended: 6 eggs by rank 3
  - +15 Pets gamepass adds 15 more hatch slots
  - Triple Hatch gamepass: 3x eggs at once
- **Charged Eggs**: unlocked at Rebirth 6
- **Golden Eggs**: also unlocked at Rebirth 6
- **Egg progression**: each area/world has its own egg machine with progressively better pets
- **RNG Egg**: special egg with RNG mechanics (RNG Egg Luck, RNG Hatch Speed, RNG Bonus Luck upgrades)
- **Farm eggs**: can grow into Huge/Titanic pets

### Pet Simulator X
- Eggs purchased with coins in each area
- Hatch rates affected by luck boosts (except Basic tier)
- Mythical Hunter gamepass boosts mythical hatch rates

### Arm Wrestle Simulator
- Eggs purchased with Wins currency
- Rarer pet = better strength multiplier

### Pet Catchers
- Uses Cubes instead of eggs (4 types)
- Lucky Cubes purchasable (x2 catch chance)

## 3. ZONES / AREAS

### Pet Simulator 99
- **Total areas**: 274 unlockable areas
- **Worlds**: 4 main worlds + 8 sub-worlds
  - Spawn World (Coins) -- areas 1-99+
  - Tech World (Tech Coins) -- unlocked mid-game
  - Void World (Void Coins) -- unlocked at Rebirth 8
  - Fantasy World (Fantasy Coins) -- unlocked later
  - Sub-worlds: Forest, Beach, Cave, Winter, Volcano, Crimson Forest, Glitch World, Prison World, Farming World, Fishing World, Obby World, Kawaii World, Enchanted Marshlands
- **Unlock mechanic**: coins/currency specific to world + some areas require quests (Prison World)
- **Currency progression within world**: Gold Coin > Gold Bar > Platinum Coin > Platinum Bar > Emerald Coin > Emerald Bar > Sapphire Coin > Sapphire Bar
- **Area features**: each unlocks new eggs, resources, upgrades, minigames, machines, boss chests
- **Theme diversity**: prehistoric jungles, futuristic space stations, haunted forests, alien zones, diamond cities, enchanted marshlands

### Pet Simulator X
- Multiple worlds with themed areas
- Spawn World > Fantasy World > others
- Enchanting Circle in Fantasy World

### Arm Wrestle Simulator
- Multiple worlds with bosses
- Unlock by defeating final boss of current world + enough wins
- Each world has unique bosses and upgrades

### Pet Catchers
- Multiple regions where pets spawn
- Magma Basin unlocks trading
- Quests/fishing/minigames in different zones

  - **Raid**: Strength, pet team size, pet luck (from Raid Orbs)

  - **Pet capacity**: collar upgrades increase pet followers

### Pet Simulator X

- Hoverboard gamepass for movement

### Arm Wrestle Simulator
- **Trail Upgrades**: Walk Speed + Total Strength bonus
- **Title Upgrades**: cosmetic showing strength level
- **Rebirth Upgrades**: cost Super Rebirth Tokens, significant boosts
- **Pet equip slots**: expandable through various methods

## 5. VISUAL EFFECTS

### Pet Simulator 99
- **Hatch animations**: Unique animation for Huge pets (all pets replaced with blue fog that transforms into the Huge). Different animations per rarity tier
- **Auras**: Rarer auras have additional particle effects and alter player animations
- **Collect effects**: Floating "+N" text in world coordinates, sell zone flash, screen flash on upgrades
- **Rebirth effects**: Camera shake on rebirth
- **Combo system**: Visual combo counter x2-x5
- **Potato mode**: option to disable visual effects for lower-end devices
- **Pet following**: pets orbit around player with visual trails
- **Zone entry**: themed visual transitions between areas
- **Breakable destruction**: coin/gem particles on break

### General Simulator Patterns (from research)
- Screen shake on big actions
- Floating damage/currency numbers
- Flash effects on purchases/upgrades
- Particle systems on hatching (escalating with rarity)
- Glow/aura effects on rare pets
- Trail effects on movement upgrades
- Rebirth screen wipe/explosion effect

## 6. MECHANICS

### Pet Simulator 99

**Rebirth/Prestige**:
- 9 total rebirths (as of Update 26)
- Keep: pets, unlocks. Lose: coins, area progress reset
- Each rebirth: +75% permanent pet strength bonus (cumulative)
- Rebirth requirements and rewards:
  1. Area 25 (Tiki): Teleport cannon, Castle Key (Trading Plaza, Spinny Wheel), 10k diamonds
  2. Area 50 (Fire Dojo): Clans, Auto Hatch, 25k diamonds
  3. Area 75 (Haunted Forest): Pet Teams, Enchant Loadout, 50k diamonds, item bundles
  4. Area 99 (Rainbow Road): Ultimates (Ground Pound), World 2 access, Mastery, 100k diamonds
  5. Area 125 (Frosted City): Secret Pet hatching, Area Quests, Shiny boost, 5 Pinatas, bundles
  6. Area 150 (Alien Mothership): Charged Eggs, Golden Eggs, 500k diamonds
  7. Area 175 (Dominus Lair): +30% Ultimate recharge, +25% Ultimate XP, +20% Huge XP, 500k diamonds
  8. Area 199 (Void Spiral): Black Hole/Void World access, Void Key, Superior Mini Chest, 500k diamonds
  9. Area 219 (Diamond Mega City): Ultra Mastery Potion (level 99 mastery), Supercomputer Radio, 500k diamonds

**Trading**: Direct player-to-player exchanges with confirmation. Trading Plaza unlocked via Castle Key (Rebirth 1). Active economy with RAP (Recent Average Price) tracking.

**Clans**: Unlocked at Rebirth 2. Donate diamonds to level up clan. Clan leaderboard. Clan battles for rewards. Member and officer slots expandable.

**Raids**: Epic dungeons with cooperative play. Progressively harder rooms with better rewards. Scheduled twice daily (10am/10pm CST). Leaderboard contests reward exclusive Titanic/Huge pets.

**Minigames**: Collection of obstacle courses + simulation games (Fishing, Digsite). 3 random minigames surged daily (3x-10x rewards). Each has its own mini-currency.

**Quests**: 3 quest machines -- Daily Quests, 3-Day Quests, Weekly Quests (in World 1 and World 2). 3-star completion for optimal XP.

**Leaderboards**: Power Leaderboard (factors in Rainbow/Gold/Shiny status, Exclusives, Huges, Titanics). Clan leaderboard. Contest leaderboards with exclusive pet rewards.

**Ultimates**: Special abilities unlocked at Rebirth 4 (Ground Pound). Recharge-based. XP-based leveling.

### Arm Wrestle Simulator
- **Rebirth**: +15% strength gain per rebirth. Rebirth rank 31+ unlocks Super Rebirth (resets wins, worlds, rebirths for Super Rebirth Tokens)
- **Auto Rebirth gamepass**: automates rebirthing

### Pet Catchers
- Quests, fishing, minigames for rare loot
- Trading (unlocked at Magma Basin)
- Boss battles (build team to conquer bosses)

## 8. ECONOMY

### Pet Simulator 99
- **Currencies per world**:
  - Spawn World: Coins (Gold Coin > Gold Bar > Platinum Coin > Platinum Bar > Emerald Coin > Emerald Bar > Sapphire Coin > Sapphire Bar)
  - Tech World: Tech Coins (same tier pattern with "Tech" prefix)
  - Void World: Void Coins
  - Fantasy World: Fantasy Coins
- **Premium currency**: Diamonds (max cap: 1,000,000,000,000 as of Update 54). Used for upgrades, enchanting, clan donations
- **Mini-game currencies**: isolated per minigame, no cross-use
- **XP**: earned through quests, diamond breakables, events, hatching eggs. Used for account leveling/ranks
- **Raid Orbs**: earned from raids, spent on raid-specific upgrades
- **Price escalation**: area unlock costs escalate per world. Upgrade costs scale with level. Each currency tier is exponentially more valuable

### Arm Wrestle Simulator
- **Wins**: primary currency from defeating bosses, spent on eggs
- **Super Rebirth Tokens**: from super rebirthing, spent on rebirth upgrades
- **Tokens**: for pet equip slot upgrades

### Pet Catchers
- Coins + premium currency system
- Cubes (consumable items for catching)

## 10. UI / HUD

### Pet Simulator 99
- **Top of screen**: Currency display (current world's coins + diamonds)
- **Side panel**: Quest menu showing active quests (Daily/3-Day/Weekly)
- **Right side**: Raid level progression during raids
- **Bottom/toolbar**: Pet inventory, Enchant loadout, Settings
- **Inventory system**: Pet grid with sorting/filtering by rarity, type, variant
- **Egg Machine UI**: Shows available eggs, hatch count selector, luck percentage
- **Trading UI**: 2-panel system (your offer vs their offer) with confirmation
- **Clan UI**: Donations, leaderboard, members, upgrades
- **Minimap/Teleport**: Portable cannon for world teleportation (Rebirth 1+)
- **Scalable UI**: game features UI rescaling option
- **Settings**: Potato mode toggle (reduce visual effects)
- **Engagement**: Visible pet count following player, DPS/CPS counters
- **Notifications**: Screen pop-ups for rare hatches, rebirth milestones, raid events

### General Simulator UI Patterns
- Currency always visible (top bar)
- Pet count / equipped count visible
- Upgrade buttons easily accessible
- Hatch animation fills screen for rare pets
- Zone progress bar or gate cost display
- Leaderboard accessible from main menu
- Shop/store button always visible
- Rebirth button prominent when available
- Daily reward popup on login
- Event banners for limited-time content

## KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR OUR SIMULATOR GENERATOR

### What PS99 does that we should consider:
1. **Multi-tier egg system** (281+ eggs across worlds) -- our generator creates 3 tiers, could scale to 5-8
2. **Pet variants** (Golden/Rainbow/Shiny) with damage multipliers -- we have species variety but no variant system
3. **Fuse Machine** (3-100 pets into 1 stronger) -- not in our generator
4. **Enchantment system** (27 enchants with leveling) -- not in our generator
5. **Multi-hatch up to 99** -- we do basic egg hatching
6. **274 zones across 4+ worlds** -- we generate 4 tiers of zones
7. **9-stage rebirth** with meaningful unlocks at each level -- we have basic rebirth
8. **Clans + Raids + Minigames** -- social features not in our generator
9. **Multiple currencies per world** -- we use single currency
10. **Mastery systems** (Fusing Mastery, etc.) -- progression depth not in our generator
11. **Game Passes** (~8 passes, 8075 Robux total) -- we generate monetization separately
12. **Huge/Titanic/Gargantuan** size hierarchy with XP leveling -- not in our generator

### Priority features to add to our simulator template:
1. Pet variants (Golden = 1.5x, Rainbow = 2x, Shiny = 3x) -- high impact, moderate effort
2. Pet fusing/merging mechanic -- high engagement, moderate effort
3. Multi-hatch scaling (3/8/15/25/50/99) -- trivial to add
4. Enchantment-like system (3-5 equippable buffs) -- moderate effort
5. Multi-currency per world -- moderate effort
6. More rebirth tiers (5-9) with meaningful unlocks -- moderate effort
7. Clan/party system basics -- high effort, high retention
8. Daily quests/rewards -- moderate effort, high retention
