Privacy Policy

AI Gold Roblox — last updated May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Gold Roblox ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our iOS application and related services.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in (Apple, Google, or anonymous guest), we receive your name, email, and a unique account identifier from the authentication provider.
  • Generation prompts and uploads. Text prompts, voice recordings, and images you submit to generate Roblox games, characters, and content are processed by our AI pipelines and stored to provide the service.
  • Generated outputs. Roblox places, models, scripts, images, and other artifacts produced from your prompts.
  • Device and usage data. App version, device model, OS version, anonymized crash reports, and feature-usage analytics.
  • Photos library access. When you use the TikTok Studio feature, we read screen recordings you select from your Photos library to trim, watermark, and re-export them. We do not upload your photos or videos to our servers.
  • Push notification token. If you grant permission, we store your APNs/FCM token to send notifications about generation status, challenges, and updates.

2. How we use your information

  • To run the AI generation pipeline and return your Roblox content.
  • To improve model quality, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To send transactional notifications (job complete, error, daily challenge).
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse.

3. Third-party services

We rely on the following processors. Each operates under its own privacy policy.

  • Google Firebase (authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging, Analytics).
  • OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini (LLM and image generation providers).
  • Meshy, Tripo, and similar 3D asset providers (3D mesh generation).
  • Roblox (when exporting content to your Roblox account via OAuth).
  • TikTok Share Kit (when you choose to share a clip; the clip is handed off to the TikTok app on your device — we do not transmit it to TikTok servers ourselves).
  • Apple Push Notification Service / Google FCM (push delivery).

4. Data retention

Generation artifacts and chat history are retained for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us at the address below; we will delete your data within 30 days unless we are required to retain it for legal compliance.

5. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, and follow the principle of least privilege for internal access. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in-app or via email.

9. Contact

Questions? Email hello@ai.gold.
